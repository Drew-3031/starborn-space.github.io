About Us

We are a student-led initiative. Here's a little about us...

"We are not figuratively, but literally stardust." - Neil deGrasse Tyson

WHO WE ARE: Starborn Space is a student-led initiative with a mission to deliver high-quality digital education content to those without access via satellites.

WHAT WE DO: It all started with a cup of coffee. Founded on February 2nd, 2020, Starborn Space has been developing a fleet of satellites to provide the people around the world with an opportunity of education. With our design, just one satellite can impact millions of people. To learn more about our initiative, you can all about it on our blog.

Our Mission

Our Mission

Our mission is to deliver high-quality digital education content to those without access via satellites.

"Education is the passport to the future, for tomorrow belongs to those who prepare for it today." - Malcolm X

As the United Nations reports, there are over 250 million children worldwide whom fail to acquire basic literacy skills. Furthermore, there are 781 illiterate adults globally, many whom are unable to provide for their families.

These are the issues we want to help resolve. We are dedicated to helping educate the world through our continued innovation and constant addition of content.

We strive to make education a possibility for everyone, regardless of location.

"The cosmos is within us. We are made of star-stuff. We are a way for the universe to know itself." - Carl Sagan

We are using several Low-Earth Orbit (LEO) satellites to be able to distribute content globally. The use of LEO satellites provide us with a low-latency but high bandwidth connection so we can provide you with the best user experience possible. The portal to connecting with our satellites will be a custom-made user-terminal, which has yet to be announced.
